Priest Incensation Rite

The priest incensation rite is a sacred act during the liturgy in which incense is used to honor and purify the priest before the reading of the Gospel. It symbolizes reverence, the sanctity of the moment, and the prayers of the faithful rising to heaven. As part of this ritual, we put incense powder on the priest for the Gospel, signifying his spiritual preparation to proclaim the holy Word.
